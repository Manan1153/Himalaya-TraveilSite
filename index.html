Leh Ladakh

There is nothing more beautful to see the heaven from the pale blue sky with full of snow and mountains of Himalayas. We will take a flight to Srinagar. You will get luxurious houseboat in Srinagar Dal Lake. On the next day, our trip continuous with the zig-zag way of Sonmarg and the final destination of the day will be Kargil. Night stay in Kargil's D'jijola Hotel.Third day of our trip will be our way to go Leh Ladakh with one stop at Magnet Hill. Next day, our travels will go ahead to Nubra Valley with some beautiful spots like Khardungla pass (world's highest motor way). Next morning, we will be headed to the Pangong Lake, and will get return to our Leh Hotel. Sixth day of our trip will be covered with the full of joy and relaxation by doing the local sitesine and enjoying the pure air of Leh. After that, in the morning, we will go to Sarchu, and will spend our cold night in Sarchu (Panga). We will be in manali next day by enjoying the activities of Solan Valley, and world's longes tunnel, Atal tunnel. Manali local sitesine on the very end of the trip. Volvo will take us to the Chandigardh next day, and then flight to our home.

Activities Included:

  • Nubra Valley
  • Pangong TSO
  • Local Sightseeing
  • Kargil
  • Snow Points
  • Khardungla Top
  • Shanti Stupa
  • Leh Palace
